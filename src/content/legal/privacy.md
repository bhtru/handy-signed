# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Your privacy is paramount. Handy ("we," "our," or "us") is designed with a privacy-first architecture for both our macOS desktop application and our website. This Privacy Policy outlines what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

## 1. Local App Operations (No Personal Data Collection)

The Handy macOS application is a local utility.

- **Offline Processing**: All core features—including file cut and paste, path copying, terminal launchers, and caffeinate keep-awake controls—run entirely on your local machine.
- **No Data Transmission**: Handy does **not** collect, store, transmit, or sell your personal data, file paths, clipboard content, keystrokes, or file metadata to any remote servers.
- **System Permissions**: Handy requests macOS system permissions (such as Accessibility and Finder Extension) strictly to perform local user-initiated actions. These permissions are never used to harvest or send user data.

## 2. Automatic App Updates

Handy may use the open-source Sparkle framework to check for application updates.

- When checking for updates, the app contacts our secure release feed to determine if a newer version of Handy is available.
- No personal user identifiers or private user data are transmitted during update checks.

## 3. Website Telemetry & Analytics

Our public marketing website uses lightweight, privacy-focused analytics (such as Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity) to understand aggregate site performance, pageviews, and usage trends.

- These tools collect standard anonymized metrics such as browser type, referring page, and page duration.
- You can block analytics scripts at any time using standard browser ad-blockers or privacy extensions.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any personal data with third parties. Any external links provided on our website (such as links to GitHub) are governed by the privacy policies of those respective third-party platforms.

## 5. Security

Because Handy processes all application data locally on your device, your files and clipboard contents remain within your macOS security sandbox and local filesystem.

## 6. Updates to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, feel free to submit an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/IsaacYeung/Handy).
